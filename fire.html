Fire

I am an advocate of "good fire" as a tool for conserving natural areas. I currently serve on the Board of the Wisconsin Prescribed Fire Council, where I collaboratively implement initiatives increasing prescribed burn capacity in the state. I previously worked as a fire practitioner, participating in over 200 prescribed burns in Wisconsin. I will be stepping down as Vice Chair this December as I transition to my new home in Washington State.
